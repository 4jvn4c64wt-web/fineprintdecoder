### Standard return window and eligibility

Nike's US return policy gives you 60 days from the delivery or purchase date. Proof of purchase is required. Nike Members get free return shipping; guest shoppers should check the return flow for applicable costs.

### What can't be returned

Several categories are explicitly excluded from Nike's return policy:

Custom "By You" soccer jerseys cannot be returned or exchanged under any circumstances. SNKRS Reserve orders are final — no returns, no exchanges. Nike Clearance store purchases are final sale. Apple Watch accessories bought from Nike have only a 14-day return window. Nike x LEGO sets must be sealed and unopened to be returned. Gift cards and items marked nonreturnable at the time of purchase are also excluded.

Hyperice items are a specific edge case: they cannot be returned at Nike stores, with the sole exception of Nike The Grove.

### Nike Clearance stores

This cuts two ways. First, anything you buy at a Nike Clearance location is final sale. Second, you cannot use a Nike Clearance store as a drop-off point for returns of items purchased elsewhere — Clearance locations simply do not accept returns.

### Shipping fees

Outbound shipping fees are not refunded as part of a return, unless you received the wrong item. The refund covers the price of the item(s) returned, not the cost of getting them to you.

### Exchanges

Nike allows exchanges through the same return flow. SNKRS Reserve orders cannot be exchanged. Custom By You soccer jerseys cannot be exchanged.
